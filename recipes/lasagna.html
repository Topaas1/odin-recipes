Lasagna recipe

Home

Description

To make a delicious lasagna of 8 servings, you need

  • 700 grams minced meat
  • 1 tsp salt
  • pinch of ground black pepper
  • 500 g tomato sauce
  • 10 pasta plates
  • 200 grams mozzarella cheese
  • 100 grams mascarpone cheese
  • 1 dl water

To make the lasagna, just follow these simple steps

  1. Cook the meat with beef fondue, season with salt and pepper
  2. Put some of the meat on bottom of a large casserole
  3. Add some mozzarella and mascarpone on top of the meat
  4. Add some tomato sauce and put 3 lasagna plates on top
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 as long as there are ingredients left
  6. Put lasagna in the oven and cook for an hour in 150 celcius

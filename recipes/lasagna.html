Description

According to Wikipedia, Lasagne is a dish of Italian origin. It is made of layers of pasta with sauce and other fillings, such as meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Pasta in plate form 500 g
  • Meat or vegetables of your choosing, approx 400 g
  • Tomato sauce 1000 g
  • White sauce 400 g or mascarpone cheese 200 g

Steps

  1. Prepare meat and/or veg
  2. After preparing the meat and/or veg, put some of it on the bottom
  3. Then cover the meat with a few plates of pasta
  4. Add tomato sauce and white sauce/mascarpone
  5. Repeat points 2-4 until you run out of ingredient

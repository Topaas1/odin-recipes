Description

This is a recipe for a delicious feta salad. Perfect lunch for those hot Summer days!

Ingredients

  • Iceberg lettuce or baby spinach 100 g
  • Tomatoes 300 g
  • Watermelon 300 g
  • Feta cheese 200 g
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the vegetables properly
  2. Cut the vegetables and watermelon
  3. Mix everything in a bowl
  4. Add a pinch of salt and refridgerate the salad for 10 minutes
